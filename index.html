About Me

I'm Ceren, an aspiring Frontend Designer with a background in law, film, and social impact projects. I translate abstract ideas into accessible, human-centered digital experiences and bring them to life through clean and structured code.

I enjoy working on digital products with social value, where I combine empathy, visual thinking, and analytical skills. With a strong interest in behavior, culture, and technology, I design and build interfaces that are intuitive, inclusive, and sustainable.

As a CMD student, I'm curious, independent, and motivated to create digital solutions that not only look good, but truly make a difference.
